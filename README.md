Since my early high school years, I have been deeply engaged with mathematics and physics, which led me to participate in prestigious Olympiads such as OBMEP and OBF. This initial exposure to structured and logical problems shaped my analytical mindset and sparked my passion for technical challenges.

I began my higher education in Electrical Engineering, where I studied for two years. During this period, I built a strong foundation in resilience and learned how to tackle complex technical problems. It was also during this time that I had my first hands-on experience with Arduino, which ignited my broader interest in technology—specifically software development.

Since then, I have focused my efforts on web development. In this role, I have consolidated practical skills in technologies such as React, TypeScript, Node.js, and PostgreSQL. Beyond the technical scope, I also actively develop skills in code versioning, team communication, and task management. I am continuously looking to expand my technical toolkit and stay up to date with development best practices.
